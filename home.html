My Story

My journey didn't start with a textbook; it started when I first looked at the stars as a kid. I've always been drawn to the infinite expanse of the unknown, which eventually led me to navigate the complex binary systems of Computer Science.


To me, a bug in a script isn't a failure—it’s an opportunity for a second chance. Just like the stories I admire where characters rewrite their fates, I find deep satisfaction in refactoring 'black hole' code into something brilliant and efficient.


When I’m not at the helm of a project, I’m capturing the universe through my camera lens. Photography is my favorite way to explore the world, and it’s a hobby that constantly sharpens my eye for UI/UX—reminding me that the perfect 'shot' (or layout) requires the right balance of light, focus, and perspective. My mission is simple: to build digital structures that are as robust as a starship and as breathtaking as a nebula .
